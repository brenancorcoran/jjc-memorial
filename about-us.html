About Us

Founded in 1985 in honor of John J. Sr. and Florence Corcoran, this annual memorial event on the first Friday in August is a day filled with golf, food, and drinks to raise mmoney for the Emmanuel Cancer Foundation.

Together with friends and extended family, John and Florence's 5 sons have continued this tradition that includes a fun filled day as well as the creation of long lasting friendships.

Participants over the years have described the event as "more of a family reunion than just a golf outing".



The Emmanuel Cancer Foundation Provides New Jersey families facing the crisis of pediatric cancer a place to turn for comfort and support through professional in-home counseling and a uniquely tailored package of assistance - all free-of-charge.

What they do:

~ In-Home Counseling
~ Material Assistance
~ Emergency Financial Aid
