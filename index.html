Dear Golfer, Guest and Sponsor,

We hope this brief note finds you all well and ready for our 38th Annual JJC Memorial Golf Tournament. August 5th, 2022 will be here before you know it!

Staying in touch and reaching out to our supporters is very important to us. This year we are increasing our communications and updating our overall JJC Memorial experience to provide you an easier and more efficient way to access all you need to know for this wonderful event.

Please mark your calendar for the “First Friday in August”…. (#FFIA)!

The last two years have been very challenging in our ever-changing world, which is why the hope, love and charity of this one small event is needed now more than ever. We sure hope to see you then!

Peace to all of our beloved family and friends!
 

The Corcoran Family
